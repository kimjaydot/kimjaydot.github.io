About Me

Kimberley Johnson


I'm an aspiring UX Designer and Researcher from Texas, focusing on user needs to drive business success. With a strong background in user research and proficiency in both qualitative and quantitative methods, I’m skilled in preparing designs for developer handoffs and conversant in front-end web languages. My experience as a former eLearning Developer has made me adept at designing for accessibility and ensuring WCAG 2.0 and Section 508 compliance.

Currently, I work at PetSmart as a Retail Sales Associate, providing excellent customer service, and I’m pursuing a Master of Science in User Experience Design at Kent State University. I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interactive Media Design from The Art Institute of Houston.

My early interest in web and digital design began during the Xanga and Myspace era, leading me to teach myself creative software and web programming languages. I have since applied my skills across various industries, including finance, marketing, beauty, and e-commerce, which has enhanced my versatility and adaptability.

I am a team player, hard-working, and thrive on new challenges. Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family, belly dancing, and snuggling with my talkative cat, Dixie.

Current Goals: Graduate in Spring 2025, land a UX Designer/Researcher role, and learn Python.
