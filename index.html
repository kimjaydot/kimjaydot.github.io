About Me

Kimberley Johnson

Welcome to my online portfolio! My name is Kimberley Johnson and I live close to Houston, Texas. I work at PetSmart as a Retail Sales Associate provideing top-tier customer service to our pet parents.

While working, I'm also a Graduate student attending Kent State University pursuing a Master's degree in User Expereince Design. I have a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Interactive Media Design from the Art Institute of Houston.

What led me into the Web & Digital field is that I was interested in creating interactive layouts for Xanga.com and Myspace.com. I studied how to use creative software from forums and self-taught myself web programming languages, that I still continuously practice learning.
